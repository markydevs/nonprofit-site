What We Do

We provide a helping hand to reach those who are encoutering a hard time whether it is financial struggles to maintain daily living, malnourished children, clothing to help families during the wintertime. We support families with hygiene kits, and funding orphans to follow their aspirations.

Our Services

Clothe cold families

We extend the warmth of love to needy families in rural upper-egypt by the free clothing from the funds received.

Feed malnourished families

We send survival food packages for families in emergency situations in rural areas, in addition to nutritious food packages to malnourished families.

Build a brighter future

Enriching needy kids' vocational skills for a brighter and happier future; building needy families from being dependent to independent.

Contribute to a healthier future

We help families in need by providing hygiene kits and resources to community-free clinics, while also connecting needy families to church cliinics and hospitals.

Why we're doing it

Every person, especially minority widows and orphans deserves a healthy atmosphere, clothing to protect their cold bodies from cold weather, meals to enrich their body, a smile and a tap on the shoulder to enrich their soul.

Currently, minoritiy widow and orphans in egypt have abundant needs and due to the lack of resources, as a result, their lives can be endangered. However with the love and support they receive under God's wing, they could be a positive light and their future can be brighter.

All for Copts assists minority widows, orphans and needy families in a biblical manner raising awareness and transforming their lives by covering basic needs.

A little about us

All for Copts is a international faith-based nonprofit organization based in the United States aimed at combatting the poverty of orphans, widows and needy families of rural communities of upper-egypt.

Who are the Copts

The word Copt is derived from the greek word, “Aigyptus” meaning Egypt. Copts are the indigenous christian population of Egypt. Today, Copts make up to 10% of the Egyptian population which can contribute to feelings of not being in the community. All For Copts takes the intiative to support needy minorites in Egypt.
