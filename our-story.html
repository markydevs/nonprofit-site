Our Story

I have always been dreaming of creating an orphange. In high school, serving at St. Mark's Coptic Orthodox church in Heliopolis, when I made weekly visits to orphanages, I saw the difficulties of many orphan children. Serving at these orphanages, I had good relationships close with many of the orphans, and discovered that many children were left in orphanges by their parents, due to the inability of their parents to provide for children. I always had a dream of changing this, and creating a safer and healther place for such kids to live, grow, and be productive in the community. Through the years, my passion for help grew and I volunteered frequently. When the pandemic hit, I started to see how it affected the kids to a greater extent, and I felt the need to establish All for Copts as a way to help not only orphan kids, but also widows, and all those who are in great need through a more organized and effective way to fulfill their needs, to give them hope and show them that there is a light at the end of the dark tunnel.

All for Copts

All for Copts is a Christian-based non-profit organization aimed to help minorities in rural upper-egypt.
