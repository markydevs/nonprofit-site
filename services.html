Our Services

We provide a helping hand to reach those who are encoutering a hard time whether it is financial struggles to maintain daily living, malnoursihed children, clothing to help families during the wintertime. Supporting families with hygiene kits, and funding orphans to follow their aspirations

Clothing needy families

We extend the warmth of love to needy families in rural upper-egypt by the new free clothing from the funds received. Clothing will not oonly cover their bodies, but will cover their soul with cheer and smiles. Some of these kids have never had new clothing during celebrations like Christmas or Easter. So you might be the reason for adding the smile to a child.

Feeding malnourished families

We send survival food packages for families in emergency situations in rural areas, in addition to nutritious food packages to malnourished families. During the holiday season, we prepare celebration food packages through your donation.

Education

Enriching kids' vocational skills for a brighter and happier future; building needy families from being dependent to independent. We encouraging families to create handcrafted items including artwork that can act as a source of income.

Health

We help families in need by providing hygiene kits and resources to community-free clinics or reduced fee clinics, while also connecting needy families to church cliinics and hospitals: in a way to prevent the spread of communicable diseases in rural areas distributing hygiene kits while raising awareness.

All for Copts

All for Copts is a Christian-based non-profit organization aimed to help minorities in rural upper-egypt.
