About

All for Copts is a international christian-based nonprofit organization based in the United States aimed at combatting the poverty of orphans, widows and needy families of rural communities of upper-egypt.

Who are the Copts?

The word Copt is derived from the greek word, “Aigyptus” meaning Egypt. Copts are the indigenous christian population of Egypt. Today, Copts make up to 10% of the Egyptian population which can contribute to feelings of not being part of the community.

All For Copts takes the intiative to support needy Copts in Egypt.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread love through God's word, while fulfilling the urgent needs of struggling families to help them survive their difficult life circumstances.

Our Vision

Better living conditions for struggling minorites in rural Egypt.
